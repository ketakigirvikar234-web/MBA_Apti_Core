About AptiCore

This site is for MBA aspirants preparing for interviews. Below are recommended steps to structure your preparation:

  1. Assess baseline by attempting one practice PDF per topic under timed conditions.
  2. Identify weak areas and create a 2-week focused study plan.
  3. Build fundamentals: core formulas for Quant, grammar rules for Verbal, chart-reading tactics for DI, and standard LR patterns.
  4. Practice with timed sets, review mistakes with detailed solutions, and simulate mock interviews.

High-priority topics

  • Quantitative Aptitude: Number theory, algebra, ratio & proportion, percentages, time & work.
  • Verbal Ability: Reading comprehension, sentence correction, vocabulary, para jumbles.
  • Data Interpretation: Charts, tables, caselets, percentage & ratio analysis.
  • Logical Reasoning: Syllogisms, puzzles, seating arrangements, series.
